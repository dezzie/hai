HUM•DAH

The Home Mortgage Disclosure Act

Consumer Financial Protection Bureau (CFPB)

Modernizing government data into useful tools for both experts and the curious.
consumerfinance.gov/hmda ·

Demonstrating its commitment to being a transparent 21st century government agency, Technology + Innovation expanded public access to mortgage lending data with an API-powered application that makes it easy to explore or download data.

HMDA is an API-powered, in-browser data query and analysis tool. It improves the workflow of those in the research and advocacy community, and may even save them a little money from purchasing statistical expensive software. For everyday citizens, it presents a relevant, low-effort data visualization experience. For devs, it demonstrates the capabilities of building a tool using our API.
